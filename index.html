Ingredients:

  • 1 pc Onion (cut into strips)
  • 4 cloves Garlic (crushed)
  • 2 tablespoon Vegetable oil
  • 1 pc Red Bell Pepper (diced)
  • 3/4 cup Water
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Cornstarch (with 2 tbsp water)
  • 1/2 Cup Carrot Blanced (Sliced)
  • 1/2 Cup Snow Peas Blanced (trimmed)
  • 1/2 Cup Young Corn (halved)
  • 1/2 Cup Canned Straw mushrooms (halved)
  • 1/2 Cup Florets of Broccol Blanched
  • Quail Eggs Boiled (and peeled)
  • 1/2kl. Chicken(breast)
  • 1/4kl.Squid/Prawn(Optional)

*You can add any other ingredients if you want.*

Procedure:

  1. Cut the vegetables into diced and the chicken into small pieces.

  2. Prepare and slice the condiments.

  3. Heat oil in a cooking pan and saute the garlic, ginger, and onions until browned.

  4. Add the chicken and cook for three(3) minutes.

  5. Add 3/4 cup of water and cook for one minute.

  6. Add all the vegetables except cabbage and bellpepper, cooking for one minute too.

  7. Mix the cabbage and bell pepper when the vegetables are slightly tender

  8. Mix in cornstarch and oyster sauce, add the quail eggs, seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.

  9. Cook until done and serve!
